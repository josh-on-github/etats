This website allows you to review states for potential travel or relocation based upon the following categories:

  1. Taxes - This category takes into account a state's effective tax burden, which is a measure of the following:
    • Income Tax
    • Property Tax
    • Sales and Excise Taxes
    • Local Tax
  2. Politics - This category takes into account a state's political affiliation, which is a measure of the following:
    • Legislative majority paired with governor control
    • Party affiliation of each state's governor, senate, and house
    • Percentage of adults who identify as a Democrat, Republican, or neither
  3. Housing - This category takes into account a state's median (most frequently occurring) home value
  4. Crime - This category takes into account a state's crime rate, which is a measure of the following:
    • Violent Crime
      • Aggravated Assault
      • Robbery
      • Homicide/Manslaughter
      • Rape
    • Property Crime
      • Arson
      • Burglary
      • Larceny
      • Motor Vehicle Theft/Damage

Use the tabs to navigate between categories.

Once you have reviewed the categories and saved your favorites, you can see your chosen states in the favorites tab.
